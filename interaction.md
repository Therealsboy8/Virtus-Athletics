# Virtus Athletics - Interaction Design

## Core Interactive Components

### 1. Product Filtering System (Shop Page)
- **Left Panel**: Category filters (Apparel, Equipment, Accessories)
- **Sub-filters**: Size, Color, Price Range, Activity Type
- **Grid Display**: Responsive product cards with hover effects
- **Real-time filtering**: Instant results as filters are applied
- **Sort Options**: Price, Popularity, Newest Arrivals

### 2. Virtus 30-Day Challenge Tracker (Community Page)
- **Challenge Calendar**: Interactive monthly view
- **Daily Tasks**: Clickable day cards showing workout challenges
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bars and completion badges
- **Community Feed**: User submissions and achievements
- **Social Sharing**: Share progress with #EarnYourVirtus hashtag

### 3. Product Quick View Modal
- **Trigger**: Click on product images throughout the site
- **Content**: Large product images, size selector, color options
- **Add to Cart**: Integration-ready for Shopify
- **Related Products**: Suggested items based on selection

### 4. Newsletter Subscription with Progress Incentive
- **Email Capture**: Stylish form with validation
- **Incentive**: "Join the Virtus community - Get 10% off your first order"
- **Progress Indicator**: Show community growth (e.g., "Join 12,847 athletes")

## User Journey Flow

### Homepage Experience
1. **Hero Impact**: Dramatic athlete imagery with tagline animation
2. **Product Discovery**: Featured products with hover reveals
3. **Community Engagement**: Challenge preview and social proof
4. **Value Proposition**: Discipline, Strength, Progress icons with animations

### Shopping Experience
1. **Category Browsing**: Clean navigation with visual category cards
2. **Product Exploration**: Filter and sort with smooth transitions
3. **Quick Actions**: Add to cart, wishlist, size guide
4. **Social Proof**: Customer reviews and athlete endorsements

### Community Engagement
1. **Challenge Overview**: Visual progress tracking
2. **Daily Participation**: Interactive workout logging
3. **Social Connection**: Community feed and achievements
4. **Motivation**: Progress sharing and milestone celebrations

## Mobile-First Interactions
- **Touch-optimized**: Large tap targets for mobile users
- **Swipe Navigation**: Product galleries and challenge calendar
- **Responsive Filtering**: Collapsible filter panels
- **Quick Actions**: Thumb-friendly button placement

## Accessibility Features
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Full site accessibility
- **Screen Reader Support**: Proper ARIA labels
- **High Contrast**: 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratios
- **Focus Indicators**: Clear visual focus states
